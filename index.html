About

Guess the music video! Inspired by Wordle, Heardle, Framed and so many others.
These are 20-ish second animated gifs of various music videos.

Resources: image height hack, calendar, modals, search as you type, and probably many others I forgot

x

How to Play

Try to guess the music video from this clip. After each guess, the video will zoom out a little.
Click the calendar icon to view past videos and your results.
Note: Currently heavily focused on 80s and 90s music videos, with a few from the 2000s.
